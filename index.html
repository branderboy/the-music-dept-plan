01 // Revenue Strategy

Four Core Engines + Expansion Revenue

The plan runs on four priced, targeted offers — with expansion revenue layered on the same rooms and the same pages.

Engine 1

Lockout Days

$250/day × 14 = $3,500

Full-day rehearsal buyouts, sold to the buyers who book in bulk: touring bands, producers, managers.

Engine 2

Monthly Artist Blocks

$600/mo × 4 = $2,400

Eight recording hours a month, recurring. Recording becomes subscription income.

Engine 3

Podcast Residencies

$400/mo × 3 = $1,200

A weekly slot, same day, same room. Podcasts book 52 weeks a year and never churn mid-season.

Engine 4

Listening Parties

$650 × 2 = $1,300

A four-hour evening event with PA and engineer, sold per release to artists and labels.

Expansion Revenue

Layered on top of the $8,400 base, using the same rooms and pages:

  • Producer Residencies — fixed weekly day
  • Podcast Packages — multi-slot network deals
  • Corporate Recording — branded & training audio
  • Voice Over — commercial, audiobook, ADR
  • Music Videos — the space as a shoot location
  • Memberships — a future phase

Recurring revenue reduces dependence on one-off bookings. A studio that starts every month at $3,600 makes better decisions than one that starts at zero.

06 // Email & Automation — Next Phase

Outreach That Never Sleeps

Email marketing and ManyChat automation roll out as the next phase — after the pages, offers, and lead capture are live and producing. The site starts collecting the email database from day one, so the list is ready when the campaigns launch.

Email Marketing — Next Phase

Campaigns by segment — artists, bands, managers, labels, podcasters, past customers, corporate clients, music schools, venues — carrying announcements, availability, new offers, listening parties, seasonal promotions, and educational content.

Cold outreach will target the area list: artists, DJs, event planners, music promoters, entertainment attorneys, videographers, photographers, managers, labels, and agencies. Every campaign lands on a dedicated landing page — never the homepage. Every reply gets pricing the same day.

ManyChat — Coming Soon

A planned automation layer for Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger, added once the pages and lead capture are live and producing. Comment or DM a keyword — "BOOK" or "RATES" — and the buyer will instantly get the rate card, the right offer page, and the booking link, with lead capture, FAQ answers, review requests, and follow-up handled automatically, 24/7.

Until then, DMs and comments are handled with saved replies and the same rule as every other channel: price and booking link in minutes, not days.

11 // Proprietary Software

Studio Growth Tools™

Custom software, developed specifically for The Music Dept. We launch with two tools — focused, not scattered. Each one captures a lead while providing immediate value.

Launch Tool #1

Missing Royalty Finder

Checks ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, SoundExchange, and MLC registrations and flags money being left on the table. It finds artists actual revenue — the strongest lead magnet a studio can offer.

Launch Tool #2

Recording Budget Calculator

Scopes a project's real cost before the first call — and lands the visitor on the Artist Block page ready to book.

No other studio in the region offers software. More tools can follow — but only after these two prove themselves in leads and bookings.
